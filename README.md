Methodology

Carbon figures represent avoided embodied (manufacturing) CO₂e — the emissions locked in when a device is made. When a device is reused instead of replaced, that manufacturing carbon is avoided. This is the standard approach defined by:


GHG Protocol - Estimating and Reporting Avoided Emissions
GHG Protocol - ICT Sector Guidance
ITU-T L.1450 - ICT Environmental Impact Assessment


Per-unit carbon factors are sourced from manufacturer Product Carbon Footprint reports (Dell, HP, Apple, Microsoft) and a peer-reviewed study in Environmental Science & Technology (DOI: 10.1021/es303012r). All figures are rounded down (conservative).

These are indicative estimates for communication, not an audited GHG inventory.
